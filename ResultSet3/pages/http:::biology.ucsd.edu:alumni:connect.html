UC San Diego Alumni

Biological Sciences alumni are part of the larger UC San Diego Alumni network.  The Triton network includes more than 130,000 alumni from the six undergraduate colleges, five academic divisions, and five graduate and professional schools.

The mission of the association is to foster a lifelong, mutually beneficial relationship of alumni and students with UC San Diego. Learn more about UC San Diego Alumni and activate your network today!
