Division Factsheet

The following information is a statistical snapshot of data representing the current makeup of the Division of Biological Sciences at UC San Diego.

Faculty

  • 86 Faculty / 39 Adjunct Faculty in 4 Academic Sections (Departments)
    • Cell & Developmental Biology (CDB)
    • Ecology, Behavior, and Evolution (EBE)
    • Molecular Biology (MB)
    • Neurobiology (NB)
  • 22 Members, National Academy of Sciences
  • 18 Members, American Academy of Arts & Sciences
  • 24 Fellows, American Association for the Advancement of Science
  • 11 Endowed Chairs and Professorships
  • 11 HHMI Investigators
  • $60 Million in Grant Support

Students

  • 4,063 Undergraduate Students in 8 Biology Majors:
    • Biochemistry & Cell Biology
    • Bioinformatics
    • Ecology Behavior & Evolution
    • General Biology
    • Human Biology
    • Microbiology
    • Molecular Biology
    • Physiology & Neurosciences
  • 168 Postdoctoral Fellows
  • 183 Ph.D. Graduate Students
  • 126 Masters Students

  • 25,000+ Biology Alumni

Facilities & Resources

  • 88 Research Laboratories
  • 1 Research Center
    • San Diego Center for Algae Biotechnology (SD-CAB)
  • 4 Organized Research Units
    • BioCircuits Institute
    • Center for Chronobiology
    • Food and Fuel for the 21st Century (FF-21)
    • Kavli Institute for Brain and Mind
  • 178,351 square feet - Research Space
  • 26,802 square feet - Teaching Labs
