micromacro

Why get a fury? Tiny with agha already has cleave(70p%), it is sub-optimal. The optimal build for tiny is drums, phase, agha, optional yasha to manta style if you need to overcome kiting and/or need to manta out of things and than a bunch of attack speed items.

Tiny has got the best late game dps in the entirety of the game, an early game presence and can survive the mid game spell bursts that teams usually churn out. Its just that he has to eschew alot to be able to maximize the timing he wants. An early game tiny can theoretically turn into a carry its just that he needs so many items to get there that the transition isn't very reliable unless he just gets that many kills so you still have to either carry or go support or mid-game snowball.

Overall, run a carry tiny if you want options and are ready to pick a lineup that'll give him both farming space & have enough disables to prevent kiting and ban the likes of shadow demon who will screw his positioning and/or his early game farm.
